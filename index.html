My Blog

BIRYANI

If there is any dish that unites foodies from all corners of India,it is Biryani.More interstingly, different regions of the country have different type of biryani,providing a smorgasbord of detectable delights to choose from.

Pani Puri

I don’t know that pani puri needs an introduction. It’s crispy, sour, spicy, sweet, and you can’t just have one.The pani, is omg amazing! It’s a combination of mint, cilantro, black salt, chili peppers, ginger, and so much more. An absolute flavor explosion.

Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji is one of the most popular Indian snacks, specially in Maharashtra. It is a delicious combination of different vegetables cooked and mashed together. Pav Bhaji is the smartest way to have all the healthy vegetables together without compromising on the taste.

Falooda

Home Made Falooda is a dessert cum summer drink with lot of edibles. This is a very popular dessert especially during the height of summers. You will find it very popular in all leading restaurants and street side vendors as well especially in top leading states.

Shahi Paneer

Mughlai cuisine consists of dishes developed in the medieval Indo-Persian cultural centres of the Mughal Empire. It represents a combination of cuisine of the Indian subcontinent with the cooking styles and recipes of Central Asian and Iranian cuisine.

Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a variation of the popular South Indian food dosa, which has its origins in Tuluva Mangalorean cuisine of Karnataka.It is made from rice, lentils, potato, methi, and curry leaves, and served with chutneys and sambar. It is popular in South India,it can be found in all other parts of the country and overseas.
